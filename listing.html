Modern Zen Heart of Orlando Downtown New Construction Home

4.5

Welcome to our newly own beautiful, cozy colonial home in a great location! A lovely room with the natural light, a comfortable queen bed and a tub in the fully renovated private hallway bathroom will be awaiting you to feel at home. And of course, everything you would need is a 5-minute drive away such as shops, restaurants, gas stations, drug stores, etc.

The space

Our clean and charming home is located in a wonderful location. Only 3 minutes away from major highways (NJ turnpike, I-295, NJ-38) ~25 minutes drive to Philadelphia, ~35 minutes to Princeton, ~1 hour to Long Beach Island or Atlantic City, ~1.5 hours to Cape May Very close to major supermarkets and shopping centers (eg, ShopRite, Wegmans, Costco, Moorestown Mall, Cherry Hill Mall, etc.) and numerous great restaurants nearby to try American, Italian, Indian, Thai, Japanese, Vietnamese or Chinese. And yes, everything you would need is a 5-minute drive away such as gas stations, drug stores, banks and even library. About 7 minutes away from a beautiful Laurel Acres Park that has a lake, fountain, kids play area, uphill, football field and more. Great for a walk, run, skating etc. in Summer, Spring or Fall. Although shops and restaurants are only a five-minute drive away, the area feels peaceful and quite.

Guest access

Guests will have access to use the common area, the bedroom and the private hallway bathroom. The kitchen is available to use ONLY the fridge, microwave and the dinning table. There is parking in the driveway.

Other things to note

1 dog (English cream golden retriever) and 2 cats on premises, but they are not allowed to enter into any of private rooms such as bedrooms.
